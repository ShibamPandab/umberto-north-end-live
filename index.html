THE BOSTON LEGACY

An Authentic Sicilian Love Story

Stepping into Umberto's is like taking a portal straight to Palermo, via Boston's historic North End cobblestones.

Since 1978, we have maintained a simple, uncompromising promise: high-quality Italian street food served in a warm, no-nonsense neighborhood atmosphere. We specialize in classic **Sicilian Pizza**—thick, airy, double-baked dough loaded with savory sweet tomato sauce and fresh premium mozzarella.

Our arancini and panzarotti are rolled by hand every single morning using the same recipe our grandfather Umberto brought from Sicily. Like the famous traditional bakeries on Hanover Street, we don't believe in fancy gimmicks. Just honest, delicious food that feeds the soul.

Opening Hours

Like the historic Sicilian bakery traditions, we open at 11:00 AM daily and bake continuously. **When we run out of fresh dough for the day, we close.** Most days, this happens around 2:30 PM. For the best experience, we recommend arriving by noon!

Important Note: CASH ONLY
